About Us

We are T3 GIS Consulting, a geospatial consulting service company that operates within Toronto & Peterborough in Ontario, Canada. We specialize in providing geospatial related services to clients worlwide and locally. We have been in service for over 4 years and have a track record of quality work for our clients. At our company, we serve a number of industries ranging from business, tranporation to natural resources management. Our team of GIS experts are the core of our organization and work together effectively to get the best results for clients. T3 GIS Consulting has built a trusted brand worldwide for developing and implementing innovative geospatial solutions and services because we can deliver both—thought leadership and technical leadership.

Our Approrach

Our team has a progressive approach in our solutions that includes the collaboration, discovery and maintenance phase.

Discovery

In this initial phase, we meet with a client to help define and clarify the specifications of the project, and confirm our credentials as the best solution for our client’s project.

Collaboration

In the subsequent phase we submit a proposal in detail, outlining the best approach to accomplish the project, with clearly defined estimates of time needed and available cost variables.

Maintenance

In the maintenance phase, when the statement of work is accepted, we iterate through the project's requirements, developing the client's highest priorities in each iteration. We also provide training for our clients’ technical staff as well as offer maintenance plans so the client has everything they need to succeed.
