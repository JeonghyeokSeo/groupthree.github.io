Community, News & Events

Having a sense of community is important wherever in life. It give us the chance to meet and learn from others, help accompish our life goals and more. At T3 GIS Consulting, our members are very active in the GIS community and participate in a variety of volunteering activities, workshops, webinars/seminars and conferences. As our team is situated in Ontario, Canada, we work to help out in our respective communities be it online or in person, we assist in whatever way that we can. Some of our members volunteer by creating content for GIS websites, perform geospatial related tasks for different mapping organizations, help out locally in GIS meetings and more.

Events

Members from our team participate in different geospatial related events held worldwide throughout the year. From the ESRI User Conference and GIS Day to BeSpatial/URISA Ontario's Annual General Meeting (AGM), our team keeps in touch with what is going on in the geospatial world and continues to learn & grow more. This is done for several reasons and we do so to stay updated with current developments in the industry and to learn/grow more in general which in turns improves our services. The following below are a few events that some of our members plans to attend.

BeSpatial/URISA Ontario's Annual General Meeting 2021

At the BeSpatial AGM Meetings, many different scopes and innovations of the geospatial world are dicussed upon by members of the BeSpatial Group. The main focus for this particular meeting will be regarding location-based intelligence, technological implementations and workforce management. Due to the ongoing COVID-19 situation, the meeting will be entirely online.

Date: May 5, 2021 (Full Day)

Location: Online Virtual Conference

ESRI's GIS Day 2020

ESRI's GIS Day is an entire day (celebrated globally) commited to teachings others about GIS and the applications it has, which is making a positive difference in society. Individuals from all over the world showcase their work, highlight new innovations, meet new people, speak on pressing matters, what to look forward in the future and more. Due to the COVID-19 situation, this meeting will also be entirely online.

Date: November 18, 2020 (Full Day)

Location: Online Virtual Conference

GeoIgnite Online 2020: Canada's National Virtual Geospatial Event

The GeoIgnite Conference is an annual event that involves the International & Canadian geospatial community and allows them to engage with each other on a variety of formats and topics. This event will feature talks from professionals from Natural Resources Canada, Statistics Canada and the Department of Fisheries and Oceans. The theme for this years event is "Leadership in Times of Disruption" which revolves around brining industry & government auhtoirty together for imperative innovations & updates in the sector. As the event is going to be 100% online due to COVID-19, the audience will be greater in comparison.

Date: July 22-24, 2020 (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM)

Location: Online Virtual Conference
