Careers

Mid-West GIS has rewarding positions available for talented, creative individuals who want to work in an environment that values innovation, teamwork, and superior customer service. At Mid-West GIS you will be able to build your future while working with people who are dedicated to growth, innovation, and the enthusiastic enjoyment of both work and life. Interested in a career or submitting a job application with our team? Awesome! Just click the email link following this message to apply. Please include the job title, your full name in the subject field and attach the following documents - resume, minimum 3 references and cover letter explaining your suitability. Also include your preferred contact method (phone or email) in the message and we will gladly review and consider your application!

Apply with us today!

Job Opportunities

As our company grows, we plan to expand our team to add more versatile expertise & knowledge. For this, we have some newly added positions that we do hire for throughout the year on a short-term and long-term basis. If you believe that you have the right skillset, attitude and knowledge in any of these particular positions, then shoot us a message and we can get connected. The following below are just a few examples of job titles at our organization.

  • LIDAR/Remote Sensing Analyst
  • GIS Database Manager
  • Cartographer
