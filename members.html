Meet the Team Members

Over some time we have become a trusted guide on GIS and geospatial related matters for a variety of organizations across the world. T3 GIS invests heavily in the professional improvement and development of our employees so they stay on top of industry, work at their best level, new software & technology and more. Our core team members come from a variety of backgrounds and have expertise in a number of fields including ecology, GIS & geomatics, earth sciences, web design programming, database management and remote sensing. Moreover, we have combined experience on several major projects throughout Canada and internationally. Kindly take some time to learn about the valuable members at our team that make us who we are! If you have any inquiries for them, please visit the help & contact page to get in touch with them.
