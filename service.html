Our Services (Intro-Type of work we do)

As our company is a GIS-consulting service company, we offer an extensive range of gis and geospatial related services to our clients. These range from network analysis to suitability analysis. Mid-West GIS is evolving as the industry leader in Geographic Information System (GIS) Consulting, Data Conversion services and GPS Data Collection. Our team specializes in RTK data collection and can offer our clients highly accurate GIS solutions. List of specific geospatial services we provide:

Software & Web Solutions

group 3 Consulting offers a variety of web based GIS solutions. Our Basic GIS Website option allows the client to have a fully functional GIS website that is developed on Group 3 Consulting server and software platform, allowing the client to avoid having to purchase those items.

GPS Data Collection and Management

Data collection is vital to the accuracy of any GIS project. Collecting new data and the maintenance of existing data are fundamental tasks for any organization which requires spatial data. That is why here at Group 3 Consulting we have the latest Global Positoning System (GPS) technology to collect data for your organization. We have the technology to collect data in any location and at any quality you would like. In addition, if you already have data we can help with its management and integrity.

Consulting & Project Management

Group 3 Consulting understands that successful GIS projects require good planning, project management and experienced staff for implementation. That is why we can help your organization deliver quality GIS projects on time and on budget. We can do this because we have experianced project managers, that can produced comprehensive project delivery solutions. This incldues but not limited to day-to-day involvement and direction, visual representations of workflow, buggeting unexpected contingencies, and proper consultation process with third parties.

Community Analysis and Mapping

Understanding the geographic distribution of the social, economic, political, and demographic patterns of your community and their relationship to services such as police, fire, schools, and government offices, housing locations, and neighborhood development are GIS capabilities that allow you to more effectively serve your community. AJD Geospatial Concepts is ready to help you with these GIS needs.

Transportation, Infrastructure, and Utility Mapping and Analysis

Group 3 Consulting is ready to help your organization with your transportation and utility mapping and analysis needs including mapping and analyzing transportation infrastructure, utility networks and infrastructure, and upgrading addressing systems.

LIDAR, 3D Modeling, and Flood Inundation Analysis

Creating 3D models from LiDAR data and vertical coordinate data and using 3D mapping to perform line-of-site and building spatial analysis, digital terrain mapping, flood inundation mapping, and overhead clearance analysis are activities essential to many GIS organizations and ones AJD Geospatial Concepts is ready to help with.

Environmental Analysis and Mapping

Knowing the natural features of your environment such as soil type, geology, hydrology, forestation, species habitation, and land cover and their relationship to the geographic distribution of population, economic infrastructure, and development will aid you in making more informed planning and development decisions. These are capabilities AJD Geospatial Concepts is ready to help with.
